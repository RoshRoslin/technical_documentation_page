How to play guitar

Want to look cool playing guitar?

  • Put on Guitar

  • Turn on Amp

  • Plug in Cable

  • Tune your Guitar

  • Rock Out!

    • Put on Guitar
  • Put guitar strap over shoulder

    • Make sure you buy a cool looking strap

    Put the strap over the shoulder of your strumming hand

    Plug in Cable
  • Put one end of the cable into the guitar
  • Put the other end into the amp input jack

    • The instrument cable should be made of high quality materials

    I prefer mogami cables

    Turn on Amp
  • Turn the power switch to "on"

    • Make sure the amp is also plugged into a power source

    Slowly turn the volume knob up

    Tune your Guitar
  • Tune to A=440

    • Or if you're playing Djent, down tune to your preferred tuning

    8 string guitars are tuned to F#BEADGBE

    Rock Out!
  • With your bad self!

    • Strum your guitar

    You're a rock star!
